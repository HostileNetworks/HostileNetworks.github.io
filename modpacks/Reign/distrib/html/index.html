About

Reign is the hallmark modpack of HostileNetworks, lovingly created and extensively worked on by HN co-founders Dulciphi and CosmicDan and contributed to by countless developers in the scene.


  • Fully-realised territory system with chunk-claims, PvP team-play or PvE "counter-op" gameplay;
  • Built around custom forks of Ancient Warfare 2, Epic Siege Mod and more;
  • Exclusive mods such as PathfinderTweaks which encourage players to make smarter solutions to problems instead of spamming or grinding;
  • [Future] Lots of lore and story! Custom NPC's and hand-crafted worldgen mechanics, contributed to by many fans of the modpack objective (hopefully);
